# saven

## Project Overview
Saven is a next-generation AI companion designed to hold space for your thoughts. Speak freely, be heard without judgment, and receive a visual and vocal 'hug' tailored to your moment of need. It transcends traditional chatbots by utilizing Google Gemini multi-modal inputs and responses in real time to meet your moment.
